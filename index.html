Cost Transparency

FTR is very clear in its communication, approach and costs. Many years of expertise learned us to evaluate, create and translate a Transformation Plan into fixed costs allowing our clients to plan and budget for change.

Depending on the scope of the Transformation Plan we offer various options:

Fixed Intake fee

Engaging into a Transformation project is an irrevocable process, a good understanding of impact, next steps, stages and costs are key drivers and mandatory for a success implementation.

  • The Intake report provides this clarity prior to committing to the Transformation Plan.
  • The Intake will be done at a pre-agreed fixed rate.

Project Plan staged invoicing

  • The Roadmap (Project Plan) will clearly indicate process, ownership, timelines and progress.
  • With a fixed fee per stage, based on completion of pre-agreed, defined next steps and/or deliverables

All-in monthly rate with success based incentive

  • If the Transformation Plan is in some cases strongly dependent on logical next steps (e.g Works Council or Trade Union related processes), as timelines are impacted hereby we can also agree on a all-in monthly fee.
  • This fee is independent of the number FTR Specialists working on the Transformation Plan

All-in fixed Transformation Plan fee

FTR uniquely offers for mid- to large sized Transformation Projects an all-in fixed fee with a success based incentive for on time and within budget delivery
It allows for clear budgetting, full transparency on costs important for share- and stakeholders.
