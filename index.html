Intake

Engaging into a company transformation is a demanding and irrevocable process once started. A good understanding of the value creation, the critical drivers for change and the impact on the organization is key for a successful implementation. The Intake report provides this clarity by:

  • defining areas for improvement,
  • mapping priorities,
  • indicating timelines,
  • inventory of (potential) risks,
  • indicating Transformation project cost and Transformation related costs

The Intake report is leading for any Transformation Project. The one-off Intake Report cost is a non committing fee, after receiving the Intake report its the client decision to enter into the next phase, kick-starting the Transformation Project, or not. FTR needs – at most - one week on-site assessment to report back on transformation changes needed to create the envisioned future organisation. Once agreed to the Transformation Plan the next step is creating a competence, capability staged assessment of the Sr. and Middle Management.

Management assessment

Present company status is a direct result of decisions taken by Sr. Management anticipating on market- and customers dynamics, economy and linked to company strategy. When a company future and results are a concern, it makes sense to assess the competence and capabilities of all managerial layers and to see if there is alignment with all in- and external stake- and shareholders.

Risk Mitigation

FTR will assess potential risks related to the Transformation and advice on mitigating actions The mitigation plan is part of the Roadmap.

Cost Transparency

FTR is very clear in its communication, approach and costs. Many years of expertise learned us to evaluate, create and translate a Transformation Plan into fixed costs allowing our clients to plan and budget for change.

Depending on the Transformation Plan scope we offer various options to best fit our client’s requirements:

Fixed Intake fee

Engaging into a Transformation project is an irrevocable process, a good understanding of impact, next steps, stages and costs are key drivers and mandatory for a success implementation.

  • The Intake report provides this clearly and understanding prior to committing to the Transformation Plan.
  • The Intake will be done at a pre-agreed fixed rate.

Project Plan staged invoicing

  • The Roadmap (Project Plan) will clearly indicate process, ownership, timelines and progress.
  • With a fixed fee per stage, based on completion of pre-agreed, defined next steps and/or deliverables

All-in monthly rate with success based incentive

  • If the Transformation Plan is strongly dependent phased (e.g Works Council or Union related processes) we can also agree on a all-in monthly fee.
  • This fee is independent of the number FTR experts working on the Transformation Plan

All-in fixed Transformation Plan fee

FTR uniquely offers for mid- to large sized Transformation Projects an all-in fixed fee with a success based incentive for on time and within budget delivery
It allows for clear budgetting, full transparency on costs important for share- and stakeholders.
